Eti-Osa Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Overview

Site Name: Eti-Osa Chamber of Commerce

The chamber operates in Lagos, Eti-Osa community. Eti-Osa is a Major Local government area in Lagos State with a population of about 300,000 people. It comprises of many big businesses.

Purpose

Eti-Osa Chamber of Commerce's goal is to provide relevant information about Lagos State and the local area to help any business, resident, and/or visitor to become more involved and connect with the community. Information about the local businesses, community events, sites and fun places, FAQ, etc will be shared. This site is built with the hope that it gets people within the community to connect and grow their businesses .

Scenarios

  • How can we be more involved with the community?
  • What are the businesses involved in the chamber of commerce?
  • How can I become a member?
  • What are the upcoming community events?
